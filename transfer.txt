## 🎯 Project #31 — What It Is

**Problem:** New Stellar users need XLM just to exist on the network (base reserve ~1 XLM), which blocks zero-balance onboarding.

**Solution:** A drop-in kit that lets a business sponsor the base reserve for new customer accounts — users start with zero XLM.

**Why Stellar:** Sponsored reserves are a **native Stellar feature** (distinct from fee sponsorship), rarely productized. No Soroban contract needed.
